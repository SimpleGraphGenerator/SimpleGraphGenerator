  • Simple Line Graph Generator

    User Guide

    1. Enter the X-axis and Y-axis ranges (min,max).
    2. Provide a title for the line graph.
    3. Enter titles for the X-axis and Y-axis.
    4. Enter data points in the format (x, y). Click "Add New Line" to add more lines.
    5. Click "Generate Line Graph" to create your line graph.
    6. Click "Copy" to copy the graph image to the clipboard or "Download as JPEG" to save it as a JPEG file.
