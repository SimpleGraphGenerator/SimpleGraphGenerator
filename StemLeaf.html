Stem and Leaf Plot Generator

User Guide

  1. Enter your data values, separated by commas.
  2. You can provide a title for your graph.
  3. Select the text color for your plot using the color picker.
  4. Click the "Generate Plot" button to create your stem and leaf plot.

After generating the plot, you can use the additional buttons:

  • "Reset" to clear the input fields and the generated plot.
  • "Copy" to copy the plot as text to the clipboard
  • Tip: you can generate multiple graphs and fit the grpah in the same image.
