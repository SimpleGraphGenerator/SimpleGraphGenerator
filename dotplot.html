Simple Dot Plot Generator

User Guide

Welcome to the Simple Dot Plot Generator!

Follow these steps to create a dot plot:

  1. Enter the data points in the, separated by commas.
  2. Provide the minimum and maximum X-axis values.
  3. Enter a label for your dot plot.
  4. Enter a title for the X-axis.
  5. Choose a background color and a text color for the dots
  6. Click "Generate Dot Plot" to create your dot plot.
  7. You can copy the dot plot as an image by clicking "Copy" or download it as a JPEG.
