Simple Bar Graph Generator

User Guide

Welcome to the Simple Bar Graph Generator!

Follow these steps to create a bar graph:

  1. Enter the maximum Y Axis value.
  2. Provide a title for the X Axis.
  3. Provide a title for the Y Axis.
  4. Enter a title for your graph.
  5. Enter data in the "Data Name" and "Data Value" fields. Click "Add New Field" to add more data points.
  6. Click "Generate Bar Graph" to create your bar graph.
  7. You can copy the graph as an image by clicking "Copy" or download it as a JPEG by clicking "Download as JPEG."
