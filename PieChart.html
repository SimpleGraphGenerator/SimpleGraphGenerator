Simple Pie Chart Generator

User Guide

  1. Enter a title for your pie chart.
  2. Choose whether you want to display percentages and values.
  3. Select a text color for your chart labels.
  4. Enter your data in the "Data Name" and "Data Value" fields. You can add more fields by clicking "Add New Field."
  5. Click the "Generate Pie Chart" button to create your chart.
  6. Click "Copy" to copy the chart as an image to your clipboard.
  7. Click "Download as JPEG" to save the chart as a JPEG image.
